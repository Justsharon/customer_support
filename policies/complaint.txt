COMPLAINT HANDLING POLICY

Acknowledgement
All complaints are acknowledged within 4 business hours of receipt. 
Customers receive a case number for tracking the resolution.

Investigation Process
1. Initial review by support team within 24 hours
2. Detailed investigation completed within 5 business days  
3. Resolution proposal sent to customer
4. Customer has 7 days to accept or request escalation

Escalation Tiers
- Tier 1: Customer support representative
- Tier 2: Senior support specialist  
- Tier 3: Customer experience manager
- Tier 4: Director of customer relations

Resolution Options
Depending on the nature of the complaint, resolution may include:
- Full or partial refund
- Replacement product or service credit
- Account credit toward future purchases
- Direct apology and process improvement commitment

Compensation Beyond Standard Resolution
For service failures involving extended downtime, data loss, or 
documented customer harm, additional compensation may be offered. 
This is determined case by case at Tier 3 or above.

Repeated Issues
Customers experiencing the same issue three or more times are flagged 
for priority handling and assigned a dedicated account contact for 
all future support interactions.
