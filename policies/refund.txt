REFUND POLICY

Eligibility Window
Customers may request a refund within 30 days of delivery for unused 
items in original packaging. Digital products are non-refundable once 
downloaded.

Refund Process
1. Submit refund request via the customer portal or support email
2. Receive a return shipping label within 24 hours
3. Ship the item back using the provided label
4. Refund processed within 5 business days of item receipt
5. Refunded amount appears on original payment method within 7-10 business days

Damaged or Defective Items
For items arriving damaged or defective, no return shipment is required. 
Customers should provide photos of the damage and the order ID. Replacement 
or refund is processed within 48 hours of verification.

Final Sale Items
Items marked "Final Sale" at checkout are not eligible for refund or 
exchange except in cases of manufacturing defect.

Refund Method
Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Store credit refunds 
include a 10% bonus and never expire.
