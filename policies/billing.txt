BILLING POLICY

Payment Methods Accepted
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay. 
Bank transfers are accepted for orders over $500.

Duplicate Charges
If you notice a duplicate charge on your account, please contact our 
billing team within 30 days. Duplicate charges are typically refunded 
within 5-7 business days. We will require the order ID and the date 
of both charges to investigate.

Subscription Billing
Subscription customers are billed on the same date each month. To pause 
or cancel a subscription, customers must do so at least 48 hours before 
the next billing cycle. Refunds for unused portions of subscription 
periods are issued as account credits.

Failed Payments
If a payment fails, we will retry automatically after 3 days. After 
two failed retry attempts, the account will be temporarily suspended 
until payment is updated.

Disputes
For disputed charges, customers should contact us before filing a 
chargeback with their bank. Direct resolution typically takes 3-5 
business days versus 30-60 days for a chargeback process.
