SHIPPING POLICY

Delivery Times
- Standard shipping: 5-7 business days
- Express shipping: 2-3 business days  
- Overnight shipping: 1 business day (orders before 2pm EST)

Order Tracking
All shipped orders include a tracking number sent via email within 24 
hours of dispatch. Customers can track orders through the customer 
portal or directly with the carrier.

Address Changes
Address changes can be made up to 4 hours after order placement. After 
4 hours, the order enters fulfilment and address changes require 
cancelling and reordering.

Delayed Packages
If a package is delayed beyond the expected delivery window, customers 
should wait 48 hours before contacting support. Most delayed packages 
arrive within this window. Packages still missing after 48 hours past 
the expected date will be traced and either replaced or refunded.

Lost Packages
A package is officially considered lost after 14 days past the expected 
delivery date with no tracking updates. Lost packages are replaced at no 
charge or refunded in full at the customer's preference.

International Shipping
International orders may be subject to customs duties and import taxes 
which are the responsibility of the customer.
